### Project Summary

A B2B customer testimonial for Epicor (Vision ERP platform). Single speaker — Alan Krasne tells the full story from company history through technology pain point to long-term partnership. Alamo Auto Supply is a family-founded business (1949) that evolved from transmission rebuilding to fleet/commercial vehicle upfitting. The video covers their 2009 decision to adopt Epicor and their ongoing investment in the platform.

### Act Structure

**Act 1 — Company & Challenge:** Company origin story (founded 1949, father's vision), evolution into fleet/commercial space, and the technology pain point — their 1986 platform was end-of-life, the developer out of business.

**Act 2 — Solution & Integration:** The decision to partner with Epicor. Everything runs on one integrated platform — purchasing, sales, accounting, invoicing, inventory. Visibility across all markets.

**Act 3 — Ongoing Investment & Partnership:** Recent additions (Indigo, warehouse management, credit card processing). Epicor's continued investment in the automotive space. Closes with confidence in the partnership and strategic alignment.

### What Worked Well

**Single-speaker arc works for founder/owner stories.** Alan carries the entire video credibly because he has deep personal history with the company and the technology decision. The generational narrative (father → son) adds authenticity.

**The "choosing a partner" framing.** The video frames the technology decision as a relationship, not a transaction. "Choosing the right partner you're going to want to work with for a long time is critical" — this framing carries through to the close.

### Rules That Emerged

**1. Single-speaker B2B testimonials need a strong personal connection to the company.** Alan works as a sole speaker because he has the founder-family authority. For single-speaker testimonials, the speaker needs to be someone who can credibly tell the whole story — not just one department's perspective.

**2. Generational business narratives add depth.** The father → son narrative gives the company story emotional weight beyond a typical vendor testimonial.

### Reference Value

Useful as a reference for single-speaker B2B testimonials, especially for family-owned businesses or long-term technology partnerships. The edit is structured as one continuous narrative rather than intercut quotes — a different approach from the multi-speaker testimonials.
